About the book

Twilight is a novel series comprising of four major books; Twilight, New Moon, Eclipse and Breaking Dawn written by American author Stephenie Meyer. The first of the series was released in 2005 and the final book in 2008. Although a few other books like; The Short Second Life of Bree Tanner (A novella which tells the story of a newborn vampire who appeared in Eclipse), the unpublished Midnight sun (a retelling of the first book, Twilight, from the second major character's point of view), and an official illustrated guide to the movie were written after 2008. The books are fantasy and vampire romance themed. They tell the story of a teenage girl, Isabella (Bella) Swan, who moves to Forks, Washington, and falls in love with a 104-year-old vampire named Edward Cullen. They are primarily written from Bella's point of view, with the epilogue of Eclipse and Part II of Breaking Dawn being told from the viewpoint of character, Jacob Black, a werewolf.

I read the four books and the Short Second Life of Bree Tanner a few months ago and they have earned a place in my list of favorite books. I absolutely recommend!
